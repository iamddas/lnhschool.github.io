Welcome to our Laksheswar Nodal High School,

We would like to take this Opportunity to Welcome you all, through our website, to Laksheswar Nodal High School, at Phulnakhra, Cuttack-754001, Odisha. It will provide you with a range of information from our vision, aims and curriculum to our policies and practice. We also include lots of information and Photographs of the work of our pupils achievement and the visits and events they take part in. Our aim at Laksheswar is to provide an environment for each and every child where they feel safe, secure, happy and motivated to learn. In this way each child progresses Academically as well as developing their own unique talents.

We pride ourselves on having a staff team committed to providing the best for every child. We encourage the children to develop confidence and Independence by ensuring positive attitudes and high standards.

 

 

 

About Us

Laksheswar School was established on 2nd August, in the year of 1967.

The founders of this educational institution are Mr. Kanhucharan Lenka who was also the former railway minister of Odisha and Late Rai Kishore Mohanty, who was the former president of the school and also was a member of school managing committee.

The School provides education from Nursery to Class X and is affiliated to the Board of Secondary Education (BSE).
